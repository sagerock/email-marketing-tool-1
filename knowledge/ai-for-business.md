# AI for Business — Video Teaching Series

## About the Series

AI for Business is a free video series by Sage Lewis at SageRock, aimed directly at small business owners. The core message: businesses that embrace AI are lowering their cost of production, and those that don't are falling behind — the gap is only going to grow. Think of it like refusing to use email or a cell phone. You can opt out, but your competitors won't.

The series covers practical ways to use AI to work smarter, cut unnecessary friction, and let AI handle the stuff you hate doing. Not trendy buzzwords — real automation that changes how work gets done.

The introductory video is here: https://youtu.be/FS9-QgQoiYs

Sage also built the AI Engagement Hub — an educational analytics platform that gives educators real-time visibility into how students interact with AI. This demonstrates what's possible when you combine AI with real business tools.

## What You'll Learn

- How to identify which parts of your business can be automated with AI
- Setting up AI tools for email automation, customer communication, and workflows
- Using AI to save time on repetitive tasks so you can focus on growing your business
- Practical demos and walkthroughs you can follow along with
- How to choose the right AI model for different tasks
- How to upload documents and let AI analyze them for you
- How to organize your AI work with tags and reflections

### Key Features

**Document Upload:** Upload PDF files directly in chat conversations. The AI can read, analyze, summarize, answer questions about, and help you work with uploaded documents.

**Project Organization:** Create projects to organize your AI conversations. Use tags to categorize by topic, task, or purpose. Add reflections to document what you learned and how you plan to apply insights.

**Analytics Dashboard:** Real-time insights into AI usage — interaction counts, model preferences, engagement patterns over time, and project progress.

**Model Comparison Guide:** A comprehensive educational resource accessible via the info icon next to the model selector. Features detailed model comparisons, token economics, citation and research techniques, AI optimization insights, and best practices for model selection and prompt engineering.

5. If you have questions or want hands-on help, reach out to Sage directly

### Step-by-Step: Getting Started on the Platform

1. **Create your account** — Go to the login page and click "Don't have an account? Sign up." Enter your full name, email, and a password (6+ characters). After signing up you'll land on your Dashboard.

2. **Join a course** — Click "Join Course" from the sidebar or dashboard. Enter the course code provided by your instructor or in the video series. Your enrollment request will be sent for approval.

3. **Create a project** — Once approved, go to your course and click "Projects," then "New Project." Give it a title and description. Projects are where all of your AI conversations live.

4. **Start chatting with AI** — Open your project and type a message. Use the model selector dropdown to switch between AI models (GPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity). You can attach PDFs using the paperclip icon and switch models mid-conversation.

5. **Tag and reflect** — After a conversation, use tags to organize your chats by topic or assignment. Add reflections to document what you learned and how you plan to apply it.

## Tone Guidelines for AI

- Be warm, approachable, and genuine
- Use plain language — avoid jargon
- Be encouraging: many business owners feel overwhelmed by AI, so be reassuring
- It's okay to be a little informal and conversational
- Be transparent that you are an AI assistant working for Sage
- Focus on practical value — what can AI actually do for their business today
- Don't oversell — be honest about what AI can and can't do
